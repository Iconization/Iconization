1. Introduction

1.1 Preamble

Art began in parallel with the genesis of creation where it has been used as a talisman to protect against nature's wrath and mysterious phenomena. Art is developed after crystallization of the religious ideas, until it became a ritual tool for practicing the worships to spread those ideas and beliefs as well. Art, in contrast to the aforementioned, later became a means of expressing freedom from ideologies and beliefs that have monopolized art and artists for many centuries through the emergence of the philosophy "Art for art's sake" or as Théophile Gautier said, "l'art pour l'art", a famous French slogan in the early nineteenth century. Art at that time was a means of expression of liberation and abstraction from all intellectual and social constraints. That role started recedes and retreats in the face of rapid technological development, to this day, where art is experiencing a state of confusion and lack of compass.

1.2 Problem background

After significant impact of globalization on mankind’s lifestyle, particularly from the mid-twentieth century through the nineties where the information technology revolution and what followed to these days was accompanied and the tremendous development of social media as its applications, in addition to the sensory means of facilities attached to its hardware/software. The upcoming technologies in the next few years of offering digital currencies linked to those applications and their impact on changing human behavior and their social activities (Lawlor, 2007). Here, we can ask; to what extent will art in general have an impact in the near future?

Especially as we are facing clearly indicators the decay of many traditional, handicraft professions, and their disappearance from the labor market in the near future (Frey & Osborne, 2013), that decay comes in favor of professions that are often associated with information technology and artificial intelligence fields (Rosa, Pereira & Ferretti, 2018). Once again, is there any chance for the art to contribute in future professions?

In order to answer a rational question like that, we must agree on a formula of the definition of Art consistent with the aspirations of the beneficiaries and the development of human perception in understanding that term, that differed on its definition and agreed on its concept of all societies, whether it was developed or not. The process of formulating a definition itself is not enough, but we must go further to the philosophy behind that definition. This can facilitate the issue for novice artists and art students to preserve the rights of the artist and their products from impurities and fake products promoted here and there as unique/authentic pieces on hand, and it helps us in evaluating the original art works on the other hand.

Globalization provided great services to humanity, but it ruined many civilization and cultural features that distinguish the identities of people, because it’s codifying communities and their lifestyles within specific ways as a language and dealing with the techniques, clothing, drinks and food habits, all these factors affect the features of the nations and homelands (Getahun, 2019). Consequently, that development was negatively reflected on art and on addressing artistic issues in a manner that adapts to the new atmosphere imposed by globalization

1.3 Objectives and goals

Filling a gap in knowledge by establishing a philosophical entity, in other words, create a cognitive institution that contributes to the advancement of the arts named iconization, to provide services like explanations term related to art as the nucleus of a project that contributes to the revival of art value, by developing the following tools:

- Art Reviewing, Criticism and Evaluation

- Art Purification

- Art Consolidation

  • Mid-term target: establishing a virtual school/institution, iconization school of art

I did not find the Contemporary Art is the best answer nor post modern or others terms that we read in art history books, for simple reason there is no recognizable art movement, we simply did not find an art movement that was able to adapt to globalization to produce an artistic mood with distinctive features that could characterize an era that spanned three decades, perhaps more (Westley & Folke, 2018). Everything around us has started to accelerate change, so why do we find a painter or sculptor today who ruminates topics and forms of application of his works from art schools spanning centuries? Understanding iconization definition and principles above are establishing a fundamental base to build the objectives on it.     3. The Conclusion

    A phrase repeated by many sons of our generation that we are lucky to be the last generation that plays in the street and the first generation to own computers and cellular devices. The fact of the matter is that this generation has a huge responsibility, which is to draw the features of the near future at the very least. As a researcher interested in the art fields, I found that the role of arts and crafts in general has receded due to the inability to keep pace with technological progress. The artist’s product has become much less than the user/operator of the machine, in both quantitatively and qualitatively, and no one pays attention to the value of the craft, which is expected in any way (Zheng & Liu, 2017). Every year there are thousands of graduates from art institutions. Most of those have talents that deserve attention, but unfortunately, they do not find opportunities worthy of their qualifications/skills. In that case they only have two options, one worse than the other, either they accept a job level lower than their peers Graduates from specific technical or administrative specializations or looking for a job that is far from art fields. This tragedy is repeated without consideration by the decision- makers, as no need for these specializations, even there is no clear job title/description for the artist’s career. The solution lies in the necessity to resurrect, purify and re-create artistic activities in ways that keep pace with what digital development has been after designing clear and specific policies that protect the artist and the artistic product to care for this category of people. They are creative and they deserve care worthy of their talents and academic qualifications. The iconization was planned to be a philosophical entity that could crystallize in the form of an artistic institution and could be called a term for an artistic movement that attracts researchers and students in the field of art working in an organized way to ensure the survival of the art message as we have known it as a message characterized above the boundaries of time and space, otherwise we will expect decay and Art corrodes in branches in the coming decades, replacing them with digital applications and software, and the artist or designer will be replaced by a user/operator without any taste or artistic sense.
